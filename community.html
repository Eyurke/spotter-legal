Community Guidelines & Moderation Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Spotter is a fitness community built on respect and accountability. To keep it useful for everyone, the following content and behavior is not allowed:

  • Harassment, hate speech, threats, or targeted bullying
  • Sexually explicit content or nudity
  • Spam, scams, or impersonation of other users
  • Sharing personal information about other users without consent
  • Illegal content or activity
  • Content promoting self-harm, eating disorders, or unsafe training practices

How to report content

Tap the ⋯ menu on any post, comment, or user profile and select “Report.” Or email us at evgenyyurkevich40@gmail.com with a description of the content and the username involved.

How to block a user

Open any user’s profile and tap the ⋯ menu, then select “Block.” Blocked users cannot interact with you or see your content.

Our response

We review every report within 24 hours. Violations result in content removal, warnings, or permanent account bans depending on severity. We will act on objectionable content within 24 hours of a report.

Contact

For questions about these guidelines or to appeal a moderation decision:

Email: evgenyyurkevich40@gmail.com
